# Interview Flow

Use this flow before generating a decision pack.

## Minimum Questions

Ask only questions that materially change the decision pack. Start with:

1. What product idea are we testing?
2. Who is the primary user, and who decides to adopt or buy it?
3. What painful workflow or unmet need does this solve?
4. What do users do today instead?
5. What is the first use case or prototype scenario?
6. What constraints matter: timeline, team, budget, platform, compliance, integrations?
7. What would make this idea worth continuing?
8. What assumptions worry you most?

## Grilling Rules

- Challenge vague users, broad markets, and unclear urgency.
- Ask for examples of real moments of use.
- Separate user pain from stakeholder preference.
- Treat "AI" as a capability, not a product strategy.
- Keep a visible list of assumptions instead of hiding uncertainty.
- If the user wants speed, proceed with stated assumptions and label them.

## Stop Condition

Proceed to artifacts when you can write:

- One clear product thesis.
- One primary persona.
- One painful workflow.
- One success metric.
- One recommendation or decision needed.
- The top 3 assumptions or validation gaps.
